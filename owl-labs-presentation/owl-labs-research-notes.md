## 3. PHYSICAL SETUP DETAILS

### Optimal Placement
**DO's:**
- Center of conference table
- Eye-level height (recommended: 3-4 feet from ground)
- 4-6 feet from participants for best view
- Stable, level surface
- Clear 360° line of sight
- Even participant distribution around the device

**DON'Ts:**
- Near windows (backlighting issues)
- In corners or against walls
- On unstable surfaces
- Behind monitors or objects
- In very large rooms (>18ft radius)
- Near air vents or HVAC systems (audio interference)

### Room Specifications
- **Ideal Room Size:** Small to medium conference rooms
- **Participant Capacity:** 8-10 people maximum
- **Table Size:** Works best on 6-12 foot diameter tables
- **Acoustics:** Soft furnishings help reduce echo

## 10. TROUBLESHOOTING

### Device Not Detected

**Problem:** Computer doesn't recognize Owl

**Solutions:**
1. Check USB cable connection (try different port)
2. Verify cable is data-capable (not charge-only)
3. Restart Owl device
4. Restart computer
5. Check for driver updates
6. Try different USB cable
7. Check USB cable length (max recommended: 15 feet)
8. For USB-C hub users: Use powered hub

**For Bluetooth:**
1. Verify Bluetooth is enabled
2. Remove other Bluetooth devices
3. Reset Bluetooth pairing
4. Move closer to Owl
5. Check for interference

---

### Poor Audio Quality

**Problem:** Muffled, distant, or unclear audio

**Solutions:**
1. Check distance (optimal: 2-6 feet)
2. Remove obstructions from microphones
3. Reduce background noise
4. Check audio settings in meeting platform
5. Adjust microphone sensitivity in app
6. Update firmware
7. Test in different room (acoustics matter)
8. Close windows/doors
9. Turn off noisy equipment

**Problem:** Echo or feedback

**Solutions:**
1. Lower speaker volume
2. Check room acoustics (add soft furnishings)
3. Ensure only one audio source is active
4. Disable computer speakers if using Owl speakers
5. Check for audio loops in meeting platform

---

### Video Quality Issues

**Problem:** Blurry, laggy, or low-quality video

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection speed
2. Use Ethernet instead of Wi-Fi
3. Close bandwidth-heavy applications
4. Clean camera lens
5. Improve room lighting
6. Adjust video quality settings
7. Check platform video settings
8. Restart Owl
9. Update firmware

**Problem:** Camera not tracking speakers

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure participants are within 18-foot range
2. Check that Auto mode is enabled
3. Remove visual obstructions
4. Improve room lighting
5. Update firmware
6. Adjust tracking sensitivity in app
7. Restart Owl

---

### Connection Issues

**Problem:** Owl keeps disconnecting

**Solutions:**
1. Check cable connections
2. Try different USB port
3. Update drivers
4. Check network stability (for Wi-Fi)
5. Reduce network congestion
6. Use Ethernet connection
7. Update firmware
8. Check power supply

**Problem:** App won't connect to Owl

**Solutions:**
1. Verify Owl and phone on same network
2. Check app permissions
3. Restart app
4. Restart Owl
5. Update app
6. Reinstall app
7. Check firewall settings

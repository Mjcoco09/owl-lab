### 10. Troubleshooting Common Issues
- **Device not detected:** Check USB/Bluetooth connection
- **Poor audio quality:** Check distance, background noise
- **Video lag:** Check internet connection, use Ethernet
- **No power:** Check power adapter connection
- **App won't connect:** Check device compatibility, restart
- **Echo/feedback:** Check speaker settings, room acoustics

---

### 11. Best Practices
- **Room Setup:**
  - Ideal room size: Small to medium conference rooms
  - Minimize echo (soft furnishings help)
  - Good lighting (avoid backlighting)
  
- **Meeting Tips:**
  - Test before important meetings
  - Keep Owl firmware updated
  - Position participants evenly around table
  - Speak clearly and naturally
  - Use mute when not speaking in large meetings

- **Maintenance:**
  - Keep lens clean
  - Avoid blocking microphones
  - Regular software updates

---

### 12. FAQ - Quick Answers
- **Q:** How many people can the Owl accommodate?
  - **A:** Up to 8-10 people in an 18-foot radius
  
- **Q:** Does it work with my video platform?
  - **A:** Yes - Zoom, Teams, Meet, WebEx, and more
  
- **Q:** Can I use it without the app?
  - **A:** Yes, plug-and-play via USB
  
- **Q:** What's the video quality?
  - **A:** 1080p HD video
  
- **Q:** Can multiple people talk at once?
  - **A:** Yes, it captures all audio sources
